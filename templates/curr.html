DEFNITIONS

For the cryptocurrency market,Bitcoin trading volume indicates how many Bitcoins (in monetery terms) are being bought and sold on specific exchanges over a period.eg Bitcoin's current price of {{curpr}}USD could have a 24-hour trading volume of $5,756,609,565 for a particular exchange.

Transaction Volume (On-chain- volume) is a measure of the amount of a cryptocurrency that is transacted on the blockchain network. They are most often the volume of digital coins transferred to exchange from external venues. It also provides an insight into the real-world usage of the cryptocurrency.

The ratio between trade volumes and on-chain volumes (Bitcoin Trade Volume/Transaction Volume) at various exchanges indicates the difference between the flow of funds in these exchanges.

A higher ratio could mean that cryptocurrency is being heavily traded on exchanges, which could be a sign of speculative trading activity.

On the other hand, a lower ratio,implies a higher transaction volume indicating that the cryptocurrency is being used more for real-world transactions(medium of exchange).This suggests a more stable and potentially growing user base for actual transactions.
